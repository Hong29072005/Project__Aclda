Being this partnership, ACLEDA Bank will assist you that require
purchasing Prudential Cambodia's life insurance in order to provide

WHAT IS A LIFE INSURANCE

Life insurance is an insurance service provides sustainable financial protection for your beloved family
or the ones who you love in case of an insured event during the policy term.

WHY YOU NEED LIFE INSURANCE
