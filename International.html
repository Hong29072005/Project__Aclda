INTERNATIONAL FINACIAL SERVICE

International Financial Servicesto support entrepreneurs* to conduct cross-border trade (buying/selling goods) online quickly between Cambodia-Singapore and other countries on the Proxtera Trade Portal

* Entrepreneurs or legal representatives of companies include

  • Micro business
  • Small business
  • Medium-size business

BENEFITS

  • Entrepreneurs or company representatives can sell/export goods or raw materials abroad by ordering from partners in the international market on the Proxtera Trade Portal (https://trade.proxtera.com)
  • Sell products/goods in the modern system with international standards.
  • High security in selling products and making payments through ACLEDA Bank's system.
  • Order products/goods or raw materials as needed, with trusted sources and international standards.
  • Utilize ACLEDA Bank's financial services, such as trade finance or loans, for immediate support

PRODUCTS/GOODS FOR SALE

A. Khmer products/goods that comply with national standards and laws of the Kingdom of Cambodia can be sold in this international market through the Proxtera Trade Portal including:

  • Spices and seasonings (peppers, chili, turmeric, coriander...)
  • Rice, honey, salt, vegetables, fruits, nuts
  • Flours, natural oils
  • Meatballs, sausage beef, pork or fish
  • Frozen seafood (clam, abalone...)
  • Instant food (tea/coffee, noodles, fried chicken...)
  • Clothing and footwear products
  • Khmer artist and pottery, furniture, etc.

B. Foreign products/goods sold in the Proxtera Trade Portal that comply with the standards and laws of each country. Cambodian enterprises can order products/goods from foreign suppliers as needed, negotiating directly with suppliers in that country.

RIGISTER

Please contact your nearest ACLEDA Bank branch or call (24/7) 015 999 233, 023 994 444 to register for cross-border trade to buy/sell products/goods through the Proxtera Trade Portalfor free of charge.

Please see user guide for on board as a business.

  • Fill out the ACLEDA Bank's account opening forms
  • Sign up on the Proxtera Trade Portal
  • Open theProxtera Trade Portal (https://trade.proxtera.com)and fill in the following information (business profile) correctly
    • Company Legal Name
    • Tax Identification Number
    • Business Registration Number
    • Type of Entity
    • Date of Incorporation
  • Members can be added as needed
  • You will receive an email from Proxtera when your account is approved

SELLING PRODUCTS

A.Customers need to create Product Catalogue in the Proxtera Trade Portal:

  • Complete seller profile: store name, store description, logo, address, certificate or license if applicable
  • Create product catalogue
  • Upload product listing
  • Describe the product details, specification, pricing, payment term, etc. (see user guide)

    • B. Customers can sell products on the Proxtera Trade Portal:negotiate with buyers, accept purchase orders, create invoices (see user guide).

PURCHASING PRODUCTS

Customers can purchase goods from abroad on the Proxtera Trade Portal:as follows:

  • Search for products
  • Negotiate with seller
  • Create purchase orde
  • Perform related tasks: request quotation, request sample, get financing, and complete cross-border payments (see user guide).
